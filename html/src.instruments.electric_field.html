src.instruments.electric_field package

This subpackage holds all Instruments measuring voltages, e fields.

field_probe represents an electric field probe. reconstructed is the electric field at every time and position of the coaxial, it is rebuilt from analytical laws fitted on the field_probe. Both these classes inherit from i_electric_field.

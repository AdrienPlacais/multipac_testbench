src.theoretical.somersalo module

Produce the Somersalo plots.

Numerical implementation of the work from Somersalo et al.[1]_.

[1]

Erkki Somersalo, Pasi Yla-Oijala, Dieter Proch et Jukka Sarvas. «Computational methods for analyzing electron multipacting in RF structures». In : Part. Accel. 59 (1998), p. 107-141. url : http://cds.cern.ch/record/1120302/files/p107.pdf.

plot_somersalo_analytical(points: str | int, log_power: ndarray, orders: Sequence[int], ax: Axes, **plot_kw) → None

Compute and plot single Somersalo plot, several orders.
